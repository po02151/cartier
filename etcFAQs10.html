  • Service instructions

    If a Cartier product requires service, you may send it directly to our local service center in accordance with the procedure below and the applicable terms of service.

    This service is only available in Korea .
    1. 1. Contact us To receive a Shipping Kit to safely pack your product, please provide the following information:
      • · First name and last name
      • · Phone number
      • · e-mail
      • · Product serial number (if available)
      Shipping kit delivery address (estimated 2-4 business days depending on your location) Our contact information is as follows:
      • · e-mail: contact.korea@cartier.com
      • · Phone: 1566-7277: Monday to Sunday 10:30 am to 19:30 pm
    2. 2. Product packaging
      When you receive the shipping kit, please pack your Cartier product, fill in the requested information, and sign the service application form.
    3. 3. Transportation
      If the shipping kit request is not completed during the period, please contact us (contact us above) so that we can arrange a pick-up service with the carrier selected by us.
    4. 4. Diagnosis and cost estimate
      Our experts will diagnose your product within one week from the date of receipt of your product. You will then receive a cost estimate stating the recommended service (including cost and estimated turnaround time).
    5. 5. Service
      Your approval of the cost estimate is required for the requested service to proceed. If you decline a cost estimate, or if you do not reply to a cost estimate within two weeks, the product will be returned to you in the condition it was received.
    6. 6. Quality check
      In the final verification process, your Cartier product is subjected to all necessary functional and aesthetic diagnostic procedures to ensure our quality standards.
    7. 7. Payment
      If the repair is completed and it is a paid repair, we will request payment (account transfer only).
    8. 8. Shipping
      After your payment, we will arrange delivery by our chosen carrier.
