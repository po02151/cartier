Payment inquiry

  • How does payment work?

    All payments at Cartier Online Boutique are processed through the KCP payment system, a reliable payment agency, and we promise a safe payment service.

  • What are the payment methods?

    At Cartier Online Boutique, you can pay by card (including credit and debit cards), real-time account transfer, and PAYCO. In the case of bank transfer, you can purchase up to 10 million won per day and use the escrow service.

  • Can i change my payment card or payment method after placing an order?

    It is difficult to change the payment card and payment method for products that have been ordered. Please cancel the product you ordered and pay again. If you are having trouble canceling your order, please contact the Cartier Contact Center (1566-7277) and we will promptly guide you.

  • When placing an order, can i use cash and credit card to order at the same time?

    It is difficult to order using cash and card at the same time. You can only purchase with one payment method when making a payment. If you want to pay for two or more products with different credit cards, please order each product separately.

  • How do i pay when ordering over the phone?

    After receiving instructions for placing an order by phone through the Cartier Contact Center, a purchase link will be sent to your e-mail address. You can pay directly through KCP according to your preferred payment method through the purchase link you received in the e-mail.

    You can pay by credit card, real-time account transfer, or PAYCO through KCP. In case of bank transfer, you can pay up to 10 million won per day.

  • Is an official certificate necessary for payment?

    An accredited certificate may be required for payments over 300,000 won through secure payment (ISP) and account transfer. For the issuance of an accredited certificate, please contact your credit card company or bank.

  • Is it possible to use escrow service when paying in cash?

    Yes, when paying in cash, you can use the escrow (purchase safety) service provided by KCP.

    * What is Escrow Service? In the case of e-commerce, it is a service for trustworthy transactions by a neutral third party intermediary between the seller and the buyer.

  • What should i do if an error occurs during payment?

    In case of payment error, please check the error message that occurs, and check whether the KCP security program is installed.

    Alternatively, please check the credit card limit and expiration date, and in the case of ISP transactions, install the accredited certificate module and check the expiration date.

    In case of continued payment errors, please contact the Cartier Contact Center (1566-7277) and we will respond promptly.

  • What should i do if an error appears in the active X control installation?

    If you choose to use ActiveX and the payment cannot proceed due to a problem that ActiveX is not installed in the KCP payment window, you can enable ActiveX filtering in the "Tools" option or click the icon in the address bar and click the No ActiveX filtering button.

  • Is it possible to issue a cash receipt?

    Yes, you can issue a cash receipt by entering the cash receipt number when paying by bank transfer.

    The requested cash receipt is automatically issued and reported to the National Tax Service, so there is no need to keep it or issue it separately.

    You can check the issuance status on the National Tax Service website (http://www.taxsave.go.kr), and you can check it 7 days after issuance.
    If it is difficult to confirm, please contact the Cartier Contact Center (1566-7277) and we will help you with the issuance separately.

  • Is it possible to issue a tax invoice?

    Due to the introduction of the cash receipt voluntary issuance system, tax invoices are not issued. Depending on the payment method, electronic receipts, credit card sales slips, or cash receipts can be substituted.
