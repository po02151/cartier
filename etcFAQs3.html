Delievery inquiry

  • How is the purchased product shipped?

    All products sold through Cartier Online Boutique are safely delivered to customers through post office delivery and Valex. Orders less than KRW 5 million will be delivered by postal service, and orders over KRW 5 million can be delivered safely through Valex.

  • How long does delivery take?

    If the product you ordered is in stock right away, it will take 3-5 business days to receive it after placing your order. (A delivery date may be added for remote mountainous areas.)

    If you receive it through Valex, it will be delivered on the delivery date promised with you.

    If it takes time to secure stock, it may take 8 to 10 business days after placing an order, and the estimated delivery date will be notified by e-mail and in the order confirmation when placing an order.

  • I want to track the progress of delivery.

    If you have placed an order after registering as a member, you can check the delivery history by logging in and going to "My Cartier > My Order History".

    In the case of non-member orders, you can check the order history and delivery progress through the link in the email that you received through "Non-member order confirmation" at the bottom of the site.

    If you receive an order through Balex, instead of providing a separate shipping tracking link, we provide 1:1 guidance to designate and guide the delivery date.

  • Are shipping charges charged separately?

    Cartier Online Boutique offers free shipping on all purchases.

  • Is international shipping possible?

    Sorry. Cartier online boutique orders can only be delivered to domestic addresses.

  • I want to change the shipping address.

    You can change the delivery address during the product preparation stage after ordering.
    Please contact the Cartier Contact Center (1566-7277) for change.
    For products that have been shipped, it is difficult to change the delivery address.

  • How is shipping when ordering multiple products?

    If you have paid for multiple products at once, we will ship the products you ordered at once. Depending on the size and category of the product, the product may be packaged separately.
    If multiple products are ordered separately (a separate order number has been created), they will be shipped by order number.

  • Is delevery by courier safe?

    Yes, all products purchased through Cartier Online Boutique will be delivered safely to you through post office courier and Valex. Orders less than KRW 5 million will be delivered by postal service, and orders over KRW 5 million can be delivered safely through Valex.

  • Is it possible to pick up orders from cartier offline boutiques?

    Cartier offline boutique pickup service is currently being prepared. We will provide detailed instructions again when preparations are complete.
