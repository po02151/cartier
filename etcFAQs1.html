Cartier product inquiry

  • All products purchased from cartier are guaranteed to be genuine.

    Cartier guarantees that all products purchased are genuine. For more information about the product you purchased here Please click to confirm.

    You can benefit from the Cartier International Guarantee on items purchased in all Cartier boutiques. The international quality guarantee can be obtained through the warranty card enclosed with the purchase of the product, and the warranty must show the contents written by a Cartier boutique, an official Cartier online boutique, or an official Cartier dealer, as well as the product unique number, date, and stamp.

  • Is it the same product sold in the cartier offline boutique?

    The products sold in Cartier online boutiques and offline boutiques are the same product, and all products sold in Cartier official online boutiques are guaranteed to be genuine.

  • How do i purchase items whose prices are not displayed on the product page of my online boutique?

    For products whose prices are not visible, you can request them by clicking "Inquiry for prices" within the product page. For more information, please contact the Cartier Contact Center (1566-7277).

  • I saw this product in an offline boutique and i want to purchase it online.

    If you know the reference number of the product you want to purchase, you can search it at the online boutique. If you cannot find the product, please contact the Cartier Contact Center (1566-7277) for detailed information.

  • I would like to consult on purchasing a diamond ring.

    Even for diamond rings of the same style, the price may vary depending on the purity and setting of the diamond. To purchase, please contact the Cartier Contact Center (1566-7277).

  • I want to buy a ring, but i don’t know the size.

    You can check the jewelry size in the "Size Guide" on the product page. Alternatively, you can check your size by measuring the size of the ring you are wearing. Go to size guide >

    For further inquiries, please contact the Cartier Contact Center (1566-7277).

  • Does your watch need regular inspection?

    There are differences depending on the customer's wearing habits, environment, and whether the watch functions are used. In general, it is recommended to check the watch once every 3 to 5 years to keep it in optimal condition.

    For details on how to care for your watch, see herePlease check in.

  • Can i get engraving service for online boutique purchases?

    Currently, online boutiques are preparing to provide engraving services. If you want engraving service at the time of purchase, you can visit the offline boutique to receive the service. For more information, please contact the Cartier Contact Center (1566-7277).
