  • Richmont Korea Cartier's Privacy Policy

    Richmont Korea Cartier Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as " Cartier ") protects personal information of information subjects in accordance with the 「Act on Promotion of Information and Communications Network Utilization and Information Protection, Etc.」 and 「Personal Information Protection Act」 (hereinafter referred to as “ Related Act ”) and resolves related grievances. In order to process quickly and smoothly, we establish and disclose the following personal information processing policy.

  • Article 1 (Purpose of processing personal information)

    Cartier processes personal data for the following purposes: The personal information being processed will not be used for any purpose other than the following, and if the purpose of use is changed, we will take necessary measures such as obtaining separate consent in accordance with relevant laws or posting the changed guidelines on the website.

    1. ① Website membership management
      Cartier confirms the intention to join the membership, identification and authentication for membership services, maintenance and management of membership qualifications, identification according to the implementation of the limited identity verification system, prevention of illegal use of services, personal information of children under 14 years of age When processing, personal information is processed for the purpose of confirming the consent of the legal representative, various guidance, handling grievances, improving website contents, and providing customized Internet services to members.
    2. ② Provision of goods or services, handling of inquiries and requests, account and record management
      Cartier processing and fulfilling purchase orders, service provision, contract/invoice delivery, content provision, customized service provision, identity verification, age verification, bill payment/ We process personal information for the purposes of settlement, debt collection, processing of customer inquiries and requests, and management of our accounts and records. Cartier processes personal information to send service-related information, including messages necessary for communication or management, such as order confirmation, and to process and fulfill repair orders and after-sales service (A/S). Purchases made at Cartier Online Boutique are governed by our Terms and Conditions of Sale .
    3. ③ Grievance handling and legal compliance
      Cartier processes personal information for the purposes of verifying the identity of the complainant, confirming the complaint, contacting and notifying for fact-finding, and notifying the processing result. Cartier processes personal data as part of its activities to prevent fraud or other prohibited or illegal activities and to maintain the security of our websites and platforms.
    4. ④ Customer management, market research and marketing Cartier processes personal data for the following purposes: · Handling of customer inquiries and requests

      Marketing contact, direct marketing and advertisement provision to customers in Cartier's business field through contact information such as advertisements displayed online or customer-provided email addresses, mobile phone numbers, phone numbers, social media platform IDs and addresses; Provision of information about products, services or events, etc.

      Conducting market research to continuously improve customer service

      Research on our website and platform to continuously improve our platform, services and other benefits

    5. ⑤ Provision of integrated membership service

      Cartier provides the membership service in terms of ① through ④ above through all channels including the website, mobile application, boutique and/or Cartier contact center. Depending on the membership service, the customer can access the services offered by the contact center as well as online, offline, through the website, mobile application, boutique or customer contact center, regardless of registration with Cartier. For example, customers who create an online account will be able to receive services both online and offline. If a customer joins our Cartier Care Program (“ CCP ”), our worldwide extended warranty program , that customer is also entitled to our membership services.

    3. ③ Information that can be collected from the Cartier Contact Center

      - Purpose of collection: customer registration, telephone consultation, repair quote guidance, product and boutique related inquiries, phone sales and security, educational or development purposes, membership service provision

      - Items: name, email address, phone number, other digital platform ID, country of residence, address/postal code, date of birth, wedding anniversary, voice recording and other personal information provided by customers

  • Article 3 (Processing and Retention Period of Personal Information)

    1. ① Cartier processes and retains personal information within the period of retention and use of personal information in accordance with laws and regulations or the period of retention and use of personal information agreed upon when collecting personal information from the information subject.
    2. ② The personal information processing and retention period for the purpose of Article 1 is as follows.
      1. 1. For website membership management, Cartier processes and retains personal information (i) upon achievement of the purpose of retaining personal information or (ii) until two months after withdrawal from website membership, whichever is earlier.
        1) When an investigation or investigation into a violation of related laws is in progress
        2) When a bond/debt relationship remains due to the use of the website
      2. 2. For the provision of goods or services, processing of inquiries and requests, and management of our account and records, Cartier will provide personal information until the supply of goods and services is completed, payment and settlement are completed, or the purpose of processing personal information is achieved. We process and retain information. However, if personal information is necessary for Cartier's accounting, or if personal information is included in Cartier's business books and important documents, it will be retained for 10 years in accordance with the Commercial Act.
      3. 3. To handle grievances and comply with laws, Cartier processes and retains personal information until the handling of customer complaints and inquiries is completed.
      4. 4. For customer management and marketing tasks, Cartier processes and retains personal information (i) upon withdrawal of the customer's consent or (ii) until the purpose of obtaining the personal information is achieved.
      5. 5. Notwithstanding the above, Cartier will retain the customer's personal information for the period required by applicable laws or government orders.
    3. ③ In order to protect the personal information of customers who have not been active for one year or more (“ Dormant Customers ”), Cartier stores and processes dormant customers’ personal information separately from other users’ personal information.
      1. 1. Whether or not a customer is a dormant customer is determined in consideration of matters such as the date of login and the date of customer service request, and refers to a customer who does not use our service for the period set in the relevant laws and regulations.
      2. 2. Cartier is classified as a dormant customer and will notify users one month in advance of separate storage and processing of personal information.
      3. 3. Separately separated and stored personal information of dormant customers will be preserved for the period stipulated by the relevant laws and will be destroyed when the period has elapsed. Personal information that has not been destroyed will be provided according to the user's request to reuse the service.

  • Article 7 (Destruction of personal information)

    1. ① Cartier destroys the personal information without delay when the personal information becomes unnecessary, such as when the retention period for personal information has elapsed or the purpose of processing has been achieved.
    2. ② In the event that the personal information retention period agreed upon by the information subject has elapsed or the personal information has to be kept in accordance with laws and regulations despite the achievement of the purpose of processing, Cartier will move the personal information to a separate database (DB) or place it in the storage location different to preserve it.
    3. ③ The procedure and method of personal information destruction are as follows.

      1. Destruction procedure
      Cartier selects the personal information that needs to be destroyed and destroys the personal information with the approval of the personal information protection officer of Cartier.

      2. Destruction method
      Cartier deletes personal information recorded and stored in electronic file form so that the record cannot be reproduced, and personal information recorded and stored in paper documents is destroyed by shredding or incineration with a shredder.

    3. ③ Physical measures: Access control of computer room, data storage room, etc.
