After-sales and customer service

  • What are cartier care services? (cartier care, international warranty period guide)

    If you subscribe to the Cartier Care service, you can extend it from 2 to 8 years at the time of purchase of the watch.

    We promise generous support and special benefits so that you can maintain the performance of your purchased product for a long time.

    You can also fill out the boutique visit reservation form so that you can visit a Cartier boutique and receive a free functional check included in the Cartier Care service.

    Product registration and detailed information herePlease check in

  • What if the purchased product needs repair?

    For repair of purchased products, visit the nearest Cartier offline boutique or CS Pickup Delivery Service(limited to watches or jewelry under 100 million won), you can receive repair service. For more information, please contact the Contact Center (1566-7277).

  • How do i repair a product i purchased online?

    You can receive the same service as offline and boutique repairs for items purchased online. For repair of purchased products, visit the nearest Cartier offline boutique orCS Pickup Delivery Service(limited to watches or jewelry under 100 million won), you can receive repair service. For more information, please contact the Contact Center (1566-7277).

  • When applying for repair, i am curious about the general cost and lead time.

    The cost varies depending on the category of the purchased product. If you contact the contact center (1566-7277) after receiving the repair, we will help you with more detailed information.

  • How can i check the progress of a repair i have received?

    You can check the progress of the requested repair in "My Cartier > Service > My Service Request History".

  • How do i pay for the repair cost?

    You can pay for the repair cost when you pick up the product at the offline boutique.

  • I am curious about the personalization service.

    Personalization service is a personalized service provided free of charge for purchased products. Personalizing services include engraving service, leather embossing service, watch wrist fitting service, and belt waist fitting service.

    Cartier Online Boutique offers leather embossing service and red box embossing service.

  • Can i receive the product to be repaired by cartier?

    Yes, only watches or jewelry products worth less than 100 million won can be delivered by courier.
    CS Pickup Delivery Service For more information, please contact the Contact Center (1566-7277).

  • Can i receive the repaired product by courier?

    In the case of products submitted to the offline boutique, we recommend that you receive the repaired product at the offline boutique as much as possible so that you can directly check whether the repaired product has been repaired.
    If you have any questions regarding this, please contact the contact center (1566-7277).

  • Can i find the repaired product at any boutique in korea?

    Yes, the repaired product can be received at Cartier boutiques nationwide. If you would like to receive it at a boutique in a different area than the boutique where you applied for repair service, you can request it separately at the time of registration.

  • How can i determine which boutique i purchased a product from?

    When purchasing a Cartier product, you can check where the product was purchased from on the enclosed warranty.
