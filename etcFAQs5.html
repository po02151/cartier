Order cancellation and exchange/refund inquiry

  • I would like to proceed with a refund

    Items purchased at Cartier Online Boutique can be exchanged/returned within 30 days of receipt in accordance with the terms and conditions of sale.

    A return request can be made directly at "My Cartier > My Order History" or through the Cartier Contact Center.

    Cartier will help you pick up the returned product, and the driver will visit you within 3 business days after requesting the return. (Returns through Valex will be notified separately by the Cartier Contact Center.) After the product arrives, the refund will be processed after the product has been inspected.

    *Notes on returns

    When returning a product, it must be unused and undamaged, and all documents (invoice and warranty) must be enclosed when shipped. Also, if the attached tag is removed, it can be considered used.

    Exchanges and returns are restricted for products of personalization services (engraving and embossing, etc.).

  • I would like to proceed with the exchange.

    Items purchased at Cartier Online Boutique can be exchanged/returned within 30 days of receipt in accordance with the terms and conditions of sale.

    Exchange and return requests can be made directly from " My Cartier > My Order History" or through the contact center.

    Purchased products can be exchanged at the offline boutique only once for size exchange. (However, it is limited to products under 60 million won, and you need to contact the contact center before visiting the boutique.) When exchanging the size of an offline boutique, please contact the contact center before visiting.
    Items over KRW 60 million can only be exchanged at online boutiques, and a separate detailed inspection may be required for exchange.

    Cartier will help you pick up the returned product, and the driver will visit you within 3 business days after requesting the return. (Returns through Valex will be notified separately by the Cartier Contact Center.)
    Refunds will be processed after the product arrives and inspection is complete.

    *Notes on returns

    When returning a product, it must be unused and undamaged, and all documents (invoice and warranty) must be enclosed when shipped. Also, if the attached tag is removed, it can be considered used.

    Exchanges and returns are restricted for products of personalization services (engraving and embossing, etc.).

  • Is it possible to cancel an order?

    In the case of an order that is in a state before delivery, it is possible to cancel the order. However, it is difficult to cancel an order after the product has been shipped.

  • Who bears the shipping cost for exchange/return?

    The Cartier online boutique offers free exchange and return services for purchases. For details, please refer to the invoice enclosed with the delivery orTerms of SalePlease refer to

  • How long does it take for the refund to be processed?

    Refund request will be reflected within 3 business days after inspection of the requested product is completed. After completing the refund request at Cartier Online Boutique, it may take up to 5 business days to restore the limit of the paid card and refund the amount by bank transfer. For more information, please contact your credit card company.

  • I applied for a return, but the payment has already been completed.

    In case of cancellation of payment by Cartier, the actual credit card company's approval is canceled and the limit is restored after 3-5 business days in accordance with the card company's membership agreement. If the card is canceled after the credit card company's billing process has been completed, the charged amount must be paid, and the credit card company will refund the amount paid the following month. For details, please contact the credit card company where you paid.

  • I want to change my return address.

    To change the return address, you can specify the changed address when requesting a return/exchange within the Cartier online boutique site.
    Alternatively, if you request a return through the contact center, please provide the changed address with us.

  • How do i make a delivery reservation for a return?

    In the case of a delivery reservation in the case of a return, Cartier assists with collection. If you arbitrarily submit through other courier companies, we recommend that you use a designated courier company because Cartier cannot track the returned goods and may cause loss.

  • I want to exchange/refund the product i received as a gift.

    In case of exchange, only one exchange of the same product size is possible.
    (Refunds will be made to the person who paid for the gift.)
